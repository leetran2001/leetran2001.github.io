FINE ARTS IS MY PASSION

It is crucially important for a Graphic Designer to have Fine Arts background


I still remember at OCAD University, After three years of studying fine arts, then I decided to take graphic design as a major program and met many talented graphic design students. I admired their works and tried to approach to them and learnt that most of them took painting and drawing classes; and, some of them took experimental arts classes to improve their design skills.

In fact, from Victorian period to our present time one could not find any good graphic designer without sharp fine arts background.
